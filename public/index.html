💰 HOW MUCH TO RISK TODAY
Why not risk everything? The most important rule in trading is protecting your capital. If you lose everything you can't trade tomorrow. By only risking a small amount per trade you survive losing trades and stay in the game long enough to find the winning ones.

The golden rule: Never risk more than 10% of your account on a single trade. With $40 that means max $4 per trade.
