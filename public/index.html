📖 THE SMC STRATEGY — 5 STEPS
1
Fair Value Gap (FVG)
Three candles where the wicks of candle 1 and candle 3 do NOT overlap. That empty space is the gap. Price always returns to fill it.
2
Liquidity Identified
Previous swing highs where stop losses cluster. Big money deliberately pushes to these levels to grab liquidity before reversing.
3
Break of Structure (BOS)
Price breaks ABOVE the previous swing high. This confirms the trend is now bullish. Do not enter before this happens.
4
Price Returns to FVG
After breaking the high, price pulls back down into the FVG zone. This is the setup zone — get ready but don't enter yet.
5
Green Candle = ENTER NOW
A green candle forms INSIDE the FVG zone. This is your exact entry. Not before. This candle confirms big money is stepping in.
